About DeepTech

A Technology is considered to be DeepTech if a considerable amount of research has gone into its development. DeepThought has researched Psychology to develop a new way of building EdTech applications.

Why These Tasks?

In order to succeed as a Frontend developer, three qualities are important- comprehension of documentation, aesthetic sense and execution of logic. These two tasks assess your ability to understand the functionality and execute with a sense of aesthetics and logic.

DeepTech Mindset

Ability to comprehend new ideas, think abstract, brainstorm more, ask questions, be excited about new possibilities through Technology.

Comprehensive Training

All DT interns are trained on the complete Tech stack, irrespective of the specialization, so everyone has a holistic understanding of the development process.

Psychological Learning Models

The interns also go through DTS socio-psychological learning models, level-5 leadership and DeepTech innovation training.
